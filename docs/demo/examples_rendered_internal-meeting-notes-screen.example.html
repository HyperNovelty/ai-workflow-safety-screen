Internal Meeting Notes Cleanup

Screen ID: internal_meeting_notes_v1 | Date: 2026-07-07

Human review: Not required by this screen

Team Context

Synthetic operations team considering whether AI can help turn public-safe meeting bullets into a clearer internal recap.

Workflow Summary

A team member pastes synthetic or non-sensitive meeting bullets into an AI tool, asks for a clearer summary, then checks the summary before saving it in the team's ordinary notes.

AI Use Case

AI may reformat public-safe bullets, group action items, and suggest neutral wording. AI may not add commitments, infer private context, contact anyone, or make decisions.

Risk Flags

  • low_consequence
  • internal_draft

Recommended Controls

  • Use only synthetic or non-sensitive meeting bullets.
  • Check that the summary does not invent decisions, owners, dates, or promises.
  • Keep a human owner for final saved notes.
  • Escalate if private personnel, customer, legal, financial, health, or security details appear.

Not For

  • private personnel notes
  • customer records
  • legal or compliance summaries
  • security incident notes
  • autonomous task assignment

Notes

Low-risk proceed-with-standard-review example. It does not remove the need for ordinary human checking before reuse.

Boundary

This screen is not legal, compliance, procurement, cybersecurity, medical, financial, employment, or production approval advice. It is a local review aid only.
