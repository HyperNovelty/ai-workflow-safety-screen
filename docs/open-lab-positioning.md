# Open Lab Positioning

`ai-workflow-safety-screen` is the AWSE-adjacent public proof repo in the Hypernovelty Open Lab footprint. It demonstrates the public-safe part of a larger idea: before an AI-assisted workflow becomes normal, write down the use case, risk flags, human review requirement, controls, and excluded uses.

It connects with:

- `agent-run-receipts` for recording what happened during an agent-assisted change.
- `source-card-schema` for preserving source and evidence notes used by a workflow.
- `hypernovelty-survival-kit` for the broader umbrella map.
- `hypernovelty-verification-literacy-kit` for teaching review and failure-mode recognition.
- `ai-school-readiness-kit` for school-specific AI readiness decisions.

## Public-Safe Boundary

This repo is a reduced educational/reference kit, not the full private AWSE prototype and not a compliance product. It excludes dashboards, private candidate data, product internals, account tooling, deployment flows, and operational automation. A passing screen means the JSON follows this format and local rule; it does not certify safety or readiness.
