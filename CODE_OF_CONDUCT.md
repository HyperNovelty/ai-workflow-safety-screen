# Code of Conduct

## Standard

Participants are expected to communicate respectfully, assume public-good intent, and keep discussion focused on practical safety screening for AI-assisted workflows.

## Public-Safe Content

Do not contribute private personal data, customer data, credentials, confidential business data, regulated records, or sensitive incident details. Use synthetic or carefully redacted examples.

## Scope Discipline

This project is a lightweight screening kit. Avoid presenting it as legal, compliance, procurement, cybersecurity, medical, financial, employment, or production approval authority.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, or remove contributions that introduce sensitive material, unsafe claims, abusive conduct, or scope confusion.
