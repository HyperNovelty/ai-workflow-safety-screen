Service Team Response Preparation

Screen ID: service_team_response_prep_v1 | Date: 2026-07-06

Human review: Required

Team Context

Synthetic service team reviewing whether AI can help prepare draft response notes for routine inbound questions.

Workflow Summary

A human receives a service inquiry, uses AI to draft a missing-information checklist and possible escalation note, then a named human writes and sends the final response.

AI Use Case

AI may summarize public-safe inquiry context, suggest missing questions, and flag possible escalation. AI may not send messages, make promises, decide eligibility, or provide professional advice.

Risk Flags

  • customer_data
  • public_posting

Recommended Controls

  • Use synthetic or redacted context during pilots.
  • Name a human reviewer for every final customer-facing response.
  • Require human approval before any message is sent or posted.
  • Escalate legal, medical, payment, employment, security, or distress signals to the correct owner.
  • Stop the pilot if customers are confused, distressed, misrouted, or given unsupported promises.

Not For

  • autonomous customer-facing send
  • legal advice
  • medical or clinical advice
  • financial, tax, insurance, or investment advice
  • employment or eligibility decisions
  • sensitive data capture

Notes

Synthetic example only. No customer records, credentials, account data, private candidate data, or production systems are represented.

Boundary

This screen is not legal, compliance, procurement, cybersecurity, medical, financial, employment, or production approval advice. It is a local review aid only.
